Species That Have Gone Extinct in India

Unfortunately, several species have been driven to extinction in India due to habitat loss, poaching, and other factors.

Species Name Scientific Name Order Phylum Year of Extinction
Indian Cheetah Acinonyx jubatus venaticus Carnivora Chordata 1952
Pink-headed Duck Rhodonessa caryophyllacea Anseriformes Chordata 1935
The Indian Auroch Bos primigenius namadicus Artiodactyla Chordata 1627
Himalayan Quail Ophrysia superciliosa Galliformes Chordata 1876

These extinctions serve as a reminder of the delicate balance between humans and wildlife, and the impact our actions can have on biodiversity.
