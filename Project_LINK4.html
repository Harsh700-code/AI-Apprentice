Efforts to Protect Endangered Species

India has been making significant efforts to protect endangered species through various programs and policies.

Key Conservation Programs

  • Project Tiger: Aimed at preserving tiger populations in India.
  • Project Elephant: Focused on the conservation of elephants and their habitats.
  • Crocodile Conservation Project: Efforts to protect the vulnerable crocodile species of India.

Protected Areas

India has established numerous national parks, wildlife sanctuaries, and biosphere reserves to provide safe habitats for endangered species. Some well-known ones include:

  • Kaziranga National Park - Famous for the Indian rhinoceros.
  • Jim Corbett National Park - A sanctuary for the Bengal tiger.
  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary - Home to elephants and tigers.
